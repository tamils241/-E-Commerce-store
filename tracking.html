Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping delivers in 2-3 business days. Next day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM EST.

I didn't receive a tracking number

Tracking numbers are sent via email within 24 hours of shipping. Check your spam folder or contact support if you can't find it.

My package shows delivered but I haven't received it

Check with neighbors or your building's front desk. If still not found, wait 24 hours as carrier updates can be delayed. Contact us if the issue persists.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International orders may take 7-21 business days depending on the destination and customs processing times.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Address changes are possible only before the order ships. Contact support immediately with your order number to request a change.
